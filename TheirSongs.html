Their Songs

BTS have inspired multiple people through their meaningful songs, they talk about how harsh life can be and how you should never give in to the pain. They talk about loving yourself no matter what, tall, short, skinny, chubby, race, it doesn't matter. BTS believe music is an universal language and through that language they pass on their positive messages.
