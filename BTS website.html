BTS are a 7 member boy band originating from Korea. BTS first made their debut in the year 2013 on the 13th of June, since then they have released a total of 14 albums. Their most viewed MV is DNA, a song released in September 2018, the number of views currently stands at 666.5 million views. Since their debut BTS have won many awards, here are a few;

  • AMA Favourite Social Artist 2018
  • BBMA Top Social Artist 2017
  • BBMA Top Social Artist 2018
  • BBMA Top Social Artist 2019
  • BBMA Top Duo/Group 2019
And the list goes on for quite a bit! Anyways, as well as winning many awards they have also collaborated with many western artists like;
  • Halsey (Boy With Luv)
  • Ed Sheeran(Make it Right)
  • Nicki Manaj (IDOL)
  • The Chainsmokers (Best Of Me)
  • Steve Aoki (Mic Drop/Waste it on Me/The Truth Untold)
  • Fall Out Boys (Champion Remix)

2 years ago on November 1st 2017 BTS had joined hands with UNICEF to raise funds for the ANTI-VIOLENCE campaign (otherwise know as the LOVE MYSELF campaign) in order to stop violence towards young children and teens. Here's a bit more about each member..

Kim Seokjin

  • stage name: Jin
  • age: 26 (4th December 1992)
  • position: Vocalist, official visual and songwriter
Jin's father is a CEO and his mother was miss Korea 1977, he originally planned on taking up acting as a career but was offered a spot in the rookie group BTS.

Min Yoongi

  • stage name: Suga (solo name AGUST D)
  • age:26 (9th March 1993)
  • position:Rapper, writer and producer
Suga had always been passionate about music, he used to make and sell music at a very young age. he also knew how to play the piano and is very skilled at basketball.

Kim Namjun

  • stage name: RM
  • age: 25 (12th September 1994)
  • position:Leader, rapper, writer and producer
RM was a very smart child growing up, getting the best test scores and even having an IQ of 148! However he wished to be part of the music industry and got into BigHit ent. and was the first member of BTS.

Jung Hoseok

  • stage name: J-Hope
  • age: 25(18th February 1994)
  • position: Rapper, songwriter, producer and dancer
J-Hope always had amazing dance skills and joined BTS as a vocalist and lead dancer but had to switch to becoming a rapper because there were to many singers. His performances on stage are extremely powerful.

Park Jimin

  • stage name: Jimin
  • age: 23 (13th October 1995)
  • position: Vocalist, songwriter and dancer
Jimin was the that kid in school that everyone loved, he had great grades, was the class president and also did ballet and taekwondo! He surprised many poeople when he suddenly mention his dreams of being an idol. He has a certain charm about him that attracts everyone's attention.

Kim Taehyung

  • stage name: V
  • age: 23 (30th December 1995)
  • position: singer, songwriter, producer and actor
V grew up up in the countryside and was raised by his grandmother, his family were always very supportive of his dreams of being an idol, and his father encouraged him to learn the saxaphone to help him having more of a chance of being an idol.

Jeon Jeonguk

  • stage name: Jungkook
  • age: 22 (1st September 1997)
  • position: Lead vocalist, singer and songwriter
Jungkook joined the band at the extremely young age of 14 and is very, very hardworking. He always delivers only the best and is always living up to his full potential, in fact he is so talented at everything that he's been nicknamed the 'golden Maknae'.
