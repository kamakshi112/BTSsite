BTS (Bangtan Boys)

BTS are a 7 member boy band originating from South Korea. BTS first made their debut in the year 2013 on the 13th of June, since then they have released a total of 14 albums. Their most viewed MV is DNA, a song released during late September 2018, the number of views currently stands at 666.5 million views.
As you may have noticed, not only are they great at singing and rapping but their dancing is impeccable too! However my personal favourite MV has to be 'Spring Day';

All thanks to their hard work BTS have won many awards since they've first debuted, here are a few of them;

  • AMA Favourite Social Artist 2018
  • BBMA Top Social Artist 2017
  • BBMA Top Social Artist 2018
  • BBMA Top Social Artist 2019
  • BBMA Top Duo/Group 2019
And the list goes on for quite a bit! Anyways, as well as winning many awards they have also collaborated with many western artists like;
  • Halsey (Boy With Luv)
  • Ed Sheeran(Make it Right)
  • Nicki Manaj (IDOL)
  • The Chainsmokers (Best Of Me)
  • Steve Aoki (Mic Drop/Waste it on Me/The Truth Untold)
  • Fall Out Boys (Champion Remix)

2 years ago on November 1st 2017 BTS had joined hands with UNICEF to raise funds for the ANTI-VIOLENCE campaign (otherwise know as the LOVE MYSELF campaign) in order to stop violence towards young children and teens.
